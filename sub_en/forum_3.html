Event Overview - 2017.11.02(Thu)

Event Overview - time, content
Time Content
09:00 ~ 09:30 Registration
09:30 ~ 10:10 Opening Remarks : Hee-jung Ahn (Governor of Chungcheongnam-do)
Congratulatory Address : Suk-woo Yoon (Chairman of Chungcheongnam-do Government)
Welcoming Speech : Chu Guohong (China’s Ambassador to the R.O.K)

Presentation : Sigon Kim (Professor, Seoul National University of Science and Technology), Song Xige (Vice President, Weihai Port Group Co., Ltd.), Hirofumi Arai (Director of Research Division / Senior Research Fellow, Economic Research Institute for Northeast Asia)
