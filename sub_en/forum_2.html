2nd Pan-Yellow Sea Forum

Date : 2016.11.03(Thu) ~ 11.04(Fri) Venue : Gongju Art Center (GOMA)

Event Overview - 2016.11.03(Thu)

Event Overview - time, content
Time Content
09:00 ~ 09:40 Registration
09:40 ~ 10:30 Opening Remarks : Hee-jung Ahn, Governor of Chungcheongnam-do
Congratulatory Speech : Seok-woo Yoon, Chair of Chungcheongnam-do Council; Sung-nam Lim, First Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs
Welcoming Remarks : Si-deok Oh, Mayor of Gongju
Keynote Speech : "Pan-Yellow Sea Peace Community from the Perspective of Peace Odyssey" by Seok-hyun Hong, Chairman of JoongAng Ilbo
10:30 ~ 11:30 Special Session : Conceptualization of the Pan-Yellow Sea Peace Community Moderator : Jeong-in Moon, Professor Emeritus at Yonsei University
Presentation :
Conceptualization of the Pan-Yellow Sea Peace Community (from South Korea's perspective) - Sung Hwan Kim, Distinguished Professor of Hanyang University and former Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade, South Korea,
Conceptualization of the Pan-Yellow Sea Peace Community (from Japan's perspective) - Morimoto Satoshi, Chancellor of Takushoku University and former Minister of Defense, Japan,
Conceptualization of the Pan-Yellow Sea Peace Community (from China's perspective) - Jingyi Jin, Professor of Peking University
12:00 ~ 13:00 Luncheon
13:00 ~ 14:30 [Main Session] Peace 1) Establishing a Network for Humanities Exchange based on the Asian Spiritual Culture Moderator : Kwang Ok Kim, First-rank Professor of Humanities and and Social Sciences at Shandong University
Presentation :
Path to Peace and Prosperity in Asia: Sharing the Value of Confucianism in Life - Yushun Huang, Vice Dean of the Advanced Institute for Confucian Studies, Shandong University,
Current Status and Challenges of Confucian Cultural Exchange in the Pan-Yellow Sea Era - Focusing on Goals and Directions of Exchange - Ho-su Chang, President of Chungnam Institute of History and Culture,
Exploring the Prospects of Cultural and Collaborative Exchange on Confucianism between Shandong Province and Chungcheongnam-do - Zheng Xiaosheng, Professor at Chinese Academy of Social Sciences,
Finding an Implementation Plan for Confucian Cultural Exchange between Chungnam and Shandong - Sang-gyun Lee, Senior Researcher of Chungnam Institute of History and Culture
[Main Session] Prosperity 2) Expanding Pan-Yellow Sea Maritime Cooperation for Peaceful Use of the Yellow Sea Region - Focusing on Marine Environment Moderator : Woong-seo Kim, President of The Korean Society of Oceanography and Senior Researcher at Korea Institute of Ocean Science & Technology
Presentation :
Joong Ki Choi, Professor Emeritus of Inha University and former President of The Korean Society of Oceanography,
International Cooperation for Biodiversity Conservation in the Pan-Yellow Sea Ecological Region - Sadayoshi Tobai, CEO of WWF Japan,
Past, Present and Future of Korea-China Joint Research on Yellow Sea - Jae Il Kwon, Senior Resarcher at Korea Institute of Ocean Science & Technology,
Our Shared Yellow Sea: In-depth Discussion on Marine Ecological Environmental Protection and the Importance of International Cooperation - Zhaohui Zhang, Deputy Director of China-PEMSEA Sustainable Coastal Management Center
Discussion : Jae-young Huh, Professor of Civil Engineering at Daejeon University
14:30 ~ 14:50 Break
14:50 ~ 16:20 [Main Session] Prosperity 1) Korea-China FTA and Globalization of Mid-sized Companies in Chungnam Moderator : Choong-yong Ahn, Chairman of Korea Commission for Corporate Partnership
Presentation :
Korea-China FTA and Globalization of SMEs in Chungnam - Do Hoon Kim, Professor of Sogang University and former President of Korea Institute for Industrial Economics and Trade,
Challenges of SMEs during Local Economic Development - Focusing on Weihai City, China - Yupu Zhao, Director of Center for Northeast Asia Research, Shandong University,
Korea-China FTA and Promotion of Market Entry in China for SMEs in Chungnam - Seung Shin Lee, Head of China Team at Korea Institute for International Economic Policy
[Main Session] Prosperity 2) Promotion of Cultural Tourism Industy in the Pan-Yellow Sea Region Moderator : Jang-woo Lee, Professor of Business at Kyungpook National University
Presentation :
Strategy for Use of Cultural Tourism Resources around Taean County and Hallyu 3.0 - Jeong-Min Ko, Professor of Arts & Cultural Management MBA at Hongik University,
How can Supply Chain Networks Enhance the Financing Capacity of SMEs? - Perspective of Supply Chain Finance - Wienpang Song, Professor at Renmin University of China,
Korea-Japan Cooperation Measures for the Use of Cultural Tourism Resources of the Pan-Yellow Sea Region - Shintaro Otani, Professor of International Tourism at Hannan University
Discussion : Yougn-Seob Kim, Professor of Architecture at Graduate School of Design, Sungkyunkwan University
16:20 ~ 16:40 Break
16:40 ~ 18:10 [Main Session] Co-existence 1) Role of Local Governments in Addressing Air Pollution Status in the Pan-Yellow Sea Region Moderator : Hyun-Soo Kang, President of Chungnam Institute
Presentation :
The Macro Structural Background of Air Pollution in the Pan-Yellow Sea Region and the Role of Local Governments in the Improvement Process - Li Wang, Researcher of Land Development and Regional Economic Research Institute, National Development and Reform Commission,
Current Situation of Air Pollution in Chungnam and the Role of the Local Government for Improvement - Sun-Tae Kim, Professor of Environmental Engineering at Daejeon University,
Current Status of Environmental Cooperation in the Northeast Asia Region - Ho Yoo, Director of International Cooperation, Ministry of Environment of Korea,
Establishment of a Cooperative System for High-Efficency Coal Power Technology for Overcoming Air Pollution in Korea, China, and Japan - Min-ho Yoon, Research Fellow of Asia Department, Japan Center for International Finance
Discussion : Jae Muk Park, Professor Emeritus of Sociology at Chungnam National University
[Main Session] Co-existence 2) Challenges and Tasks of Local Governments for Energy Policy Transition Moderator : Yong Gu Yoo, Head of Policy Planning Department at Chungnam Techno Park
Presentation :
Japan's Energy Policy and the Outlook for Hydrogen Energy - Hirohisa Uchida, Professor of Nuclear Engineering at Tokai University,
Policy Direction of Solar Energy Industry in Chungnam - Jun Tae Kim, Professor of Kongju National University,
Current Status of Hyundai Motor's Eco-friendly Vehicle Development - Cheol-Ho Lim, Senior Researcher at Hyundai Motor
Discussion : Sang-Yang Noh, Director of New and Renewable Energy Center of Korea Energy Agency

Event Overview - time, content, place
Time Content
