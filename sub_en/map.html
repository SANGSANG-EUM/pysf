Event Introduction

Direction

Event Address

Address. 400, Baekjemun-ro, Gyuam-myeon, Buyeo-gun, Chungcheongnam-do, Republic of Korea

Telephone. 041-939-1000

Directions

By car

  • Seoul (Gyeongbu Expressway)
    Seoul → Gyeongbu Expressway → Cheonan Junction → Cheonan-Nonsan Expressway → Seogongju Junction → Gongju-Seocheon Expressway → Buyeo Interchange → Lotte Resort Buyeo (approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes travel time)
  • Seoul (Seohaean Expressway)
    Seoul → Seohaean Expressway → Dangjin Junction → Dangjin Expressway → Seogongju Junction → Gongju-Seocheon Expressway → Buyeo Interchange → Lotte Resort Buyeo (approximately 2 hours travel time)
※ Travel time may vary depending on traffic volume

By bus

  • From Seoul
    Nambu Bus Terminal (02-551-8550) → Buyeo Intercity Bus Terminal (approximately 2 hours travel time)
    Dong Seoul Terminal (02-446-8000) → Buyeo Intercity Bus Terminal (approximately 2 hours and 40 minutes travel time)
  • From Cheonan
    Cheonan Intercity Bus Terminal (041-640-6400) → Buyeo Intercity Bus Terminal (approximately 2 hours travel time)
  • From Daejeon
    Seobu Intercity Bus Terminal (042-584-1615) → Buyeo Intercity Bus Terminal (approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes travel time)
  • From Cheongju
    Seobu Intercity Bus Terminal (1688-4321) → Buyeo Intercity Bus Terminal (approximately 3 hours and 10 minutes travel time)
  • Rural Bus
    Buyeo Intercity Bus Terminal: no. 403 (Buyeo Market) → Korea National University of Cultural Heritage (approximately 20 minutes travel time)
    * Rural bus schedule inquiry: 041-843-6092~3
※ For reservations or further details, please inquire via phone or visit our website.

By train

  • From Seoul
    Yongsan Station → Nonsan Station (approximately 1 hour and 34 minutes travel time)
    Yongsan Station → Gongju Station (approximately 59 minutes travel time)
  • From Daegu
    Daegu Station, Dongdaegu Station → Daejeon Station (approximately 40 minutes)
  • From Busan
    Busan Station → Daejeon Station (approximately 2 hours)
  • From Gwangju
    Gwangju Songjeong Station → Nonsan Station (approximately 53 minutes)
    Gwangju Songjeong Station → Gongju Station (approximately 45 minutes)
※ Travel time is based on the fastest KTX
※ After arriving at the destination station, transfer to Buyeo Intercity Bus Terminal and then use public transportation to reach Lotte Resort Buyeo.
※ For reservations or further information, you can make inquiries thorugh the train reservation button at the upper right corner, the Korail website, or by phone
