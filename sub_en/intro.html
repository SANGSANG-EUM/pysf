Event Theme

Bridging the Pan-Yellow Sea: Driving the Great Transformation in Marine Cultural Tourism

    Marine Belt | Cruise trips and exchange of marine cultures among countries around the Pan-Yellow Sea
  • Session2
    Cultural Tourism | Seeking measures to foster a tourism ecosystem by leveraging the cultural heritages of each region

    Carbon Neutrality | Measures to achieve carbon neutrality through expansion of offshore wind farms
